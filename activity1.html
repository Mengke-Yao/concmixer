Concrete Mixing Plant Workflow and Operating Precautions

Workflow Steps:

1. Power on the mixer control system, entering the human-machine dialogue interface.
2. System initialization, including formula number, concrete grade, slump, production volume.
3. Check each silo and measuring hopper based on weight, indicating empty or full status.
4. Start the sand and gravel belt motor to feed into the measuring hopper.
5. Open the butterfly valves of fly ash and cement silos.
6. Activate the screw conveyor motor to transport fly ash and cement to the measuring hopper.
7. Open control valves for the water tank and admixture pool, allowing contents into the measuring hopper.
8. After meeting set measurement requirements, open the hopper gate.
9. Ingredients enter the mixer for blending until the set time, then concrete is discharged into the mixing truck.

Operating Precautions:

Inspect the mixing plant's components before starting, including lubrication and water pump checks.
Adhere to the correct operating sequence to prevent issues during mixing.

Pre-Operational Preparations:

Ensure the mixer and supporting mechanisms are free from obstructions.
Verify oil levels and cleanliness in the gearbox, and ensure structural stability of the mixing platform.
Check wire rope and drum winding on lifting hoppers or scrapers for correct installation.

Operational Precautions:

After machinery start-up, inspect operations and confirm the correct rotation direction.
Avoid stopping the mixer when fully loaded to prevent malfunctions or blockages.

Post-Operational Precautions:

After operations, wait for cleaning or maintenance notification before cutting power.
Ensure all cement is conveyed out of the screw pipe during cleaning.
In freezing conditions, drain all water from pumps and tanks to prevent damage.
Note: This document provides a simplified overview. Always consult the detailed manual for comprehensive instructions.
